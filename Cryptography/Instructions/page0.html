Casting and Modulus


Also, codemaking and codebreaking

In lecture, the Udacity folks talk about casting as converting one item into another. If you want to force a double to act as an int, you put the type in parentheses in front. Here's an example.

double lemonMeringuePie = 3.141519;
int newPie = (int) lemonMeringuePie; // But...newPie is just 3. The decimal part is dropped.
